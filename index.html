Key Features:

  • Burst or leaking pipes:

    • Pipes can fail due to pipe corrosion, freezing temperatures, or poor installation. Water pressure surges can also cause sudden ruptures. When this happens, water can flood walls floors, and ceilings within minutes.

  • Appliance failures:

    • Malfunctioning dishwashers, washing machines, refrigerators, HVAC systems, or water heaters can all leak—often without warning. Even a minor drip can escalate into a major issue.

  • Natural disasters:

    • Heavy rains and floods can overwhelm drainage systems and seep into basements or crawl spaces. When floodwaters aren’t promptly and properly removed, they contaminate interiors.

  • Roof leaks:

    • Damaged shingles, flashing, or gutters can allow rain or melting snow to seep through the roof, damaging ceilings, insulation, and structural supports.
